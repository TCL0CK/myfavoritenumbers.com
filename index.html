bio
My Favorite Numbers is a rock band led by Thomas McCloskey with a rotating lineup of his favorites on bass and drums --
the friends he made in fellow jazz school graduates from the Rochester, NY; Columbus, OH; and Philadelphia, PA areas.
With this project, the band hopes to constellate all of their influences into a musical voice of their own,
starting with their childhood roots in rock music.
